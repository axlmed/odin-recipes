Pollo con Tajadas

Fried Chicken with Fried Plantain Chips

A typical Honduran dish that can be found throughout the country. It consists of deep fried chicken, deep fried plantains accompanied by a salad and a special home-made sauce. Avocado and onions soaked in vinegar gives the dish an exquisite flavor. Grab a drink and enjoy the day as you feast on this delicious home-made platter.

Ingredients

  • 2lb Chicken
  • Chicken Flavored Bouillon
  • Spices
  • Vinegar
  • 2 Green Plantain
  • Cabbage
  • 1 Green Pepper
  • 2 Tomatoes
  • 1 Onion
  • Fine Coriander
  • 3 Cups of Oil for Frying
  • Salt to Taste

Steps

  1. The chicken is cut into normal portions.
  2. It is seasoned with the consommé, spices and a tablespoon of vinegar, salt to taste, and let it rest for 10 minutes.
  3. Then the oil is heated in a frying pan
  4. Flour is prepared on a plate and the chicken is marinated with the flour
  5. Fry until golden
Preparation of Plantains
  1. The green plantains are peeled and cut into slices or sliced ​​leaves for frying.
  2. Then the chimol is prepared so that the tomatoes are cut into small squares, the onion as well and the chili as well
  3. In a container, combine and add vinegar to taste or lemon, salt and pepper and the well-cooked fine culantillo
  4. Stir, then chop cabbage in a separate container.
